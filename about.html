About

New York Nonprofit Media is the must-read news source for New York’s nonprofits. The publication has served the industry for 12 years as New York Nonprofit Press under the leadership of founder and editor Fred Scaglione. City and State NY, LLC acquired the company in 2014 and, with the guidance of Fred Scaglione, redesigned and relaunched it as New York Nonprofit Media, with four distinct divisions.

  • NYN Review is the print publication of NYN Media which publishes 10 times per year and is sent to 6,000 nonprofit agencies, 360 public libraries, dozens of graduate schools, and all New York State and City elected government officials. The redesigned publication offers increased timely news articles, new sections, and preserves the human services perspective our readers rely on.

  • NYN offers numerous digital platforms. The daily e-newsletter, NYN Daily is delivered to over 20,000 opt-in subscribers and has an average open rate of 25%. The completely redesigned NYNmedia.com features increased editorial content and a new sleek design.

  • NYN Careers is the go-to career center for New York’s nonprofit industry. Featuring 1,000s of jobs each year, NYN Careers helps large and small nonprofits alike fill positions ranging from Executive Directors to human resources staffers. Feature your employment opportunity across our platforms to reach our highly targeted audience to ensure you hear from the most qualified applicants.

  • NYN Events feature NYN-hosted events as well as other nonprofit events throughout the state. NYN Events offer you the opportunity to mix and mingle with top industry and government officials. Visit our events page to learn more about 2015 events throughout New York or to list your organization’s event.
